INTRODUCTION TO

I'm a Computer Science student who discovered graphic design as a creative outlet and found my passion lies at the intersection of both worlds.

Currently in my junior year, I spend my days writing algorithms and my evenings crafting visual narratives. What started as curiosity about design has evolved into a genuine love for creating experiences that are both functionally elegant and visually compelling.

My approach blends the systematic thinking of programming with the intuitive exploration of design. Every project is an opportunity to solve problems creatively, whether through clean code or thoughtful visual communication.
